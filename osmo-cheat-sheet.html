Shutter/Record Button
Middle right side
  • Press Once: Take photo / Start or stop video recording
  • Press & Hold: Burst shooting mode (photo mode only)
Switch Button
Lower right side
  • Press Once: Switch between front/rear cameras
  • Press 2x: Toggle landscape/portrait orientation
  • Press 3x: Switch photo/video mode (or Quick Settings if customized)
Trigger
Front grip
  • Press Once: Start/stop ActiveTrack 5.0
  • Press 2x: Recenter gimbal to camera view
  • Press & Hold: Lock mode (gimbal won't follow movements)
  • Press + Hold: Fast follow speed mode
Joystick
Upper left side
  • Move Up/Down: Control gimbal tilt
  • Move Left/Right: Control gimbal pan
    (In SpinShot: controls roll axis)
  • In App Menus: Navigate settings and options
Zoom Slider
Left side below joystick
  • Push to +: Zoom in (enlarge subject)
  • Push to -: Zoom out (shrink subject)
  • Single Push: Single zoom increment
  • Continuous Push: Smooth zoom adjustment
